Introduction

This project leverages Hybrid Intelligence and the AI-TPACK framework to transform teaching concepts into interactive web simulations, enabling cost-effective interdisciplinary STEM learning with drone technology while fostering spatial reasoning, critical thinking, and computational skills.

Challenges Resolved by AI-Teacher Collaborative Generated Simulation

  • Spatial understanding: Struggled with 3D concepts and flight dynamics.
  • Legal limitations: Altitude rules hindered trigonometric measurements.
  • Navigation visualization: Issues visualizing paths from code.
